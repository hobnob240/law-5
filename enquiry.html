Frequently asked questions

A few common questions before you send an enquiry.

Do I need to know exact paint colours before asking for a quote?

No. It is enough to explain the job, the rooms or surfaces involved and the type of result you want.

Can I enquire about a small job?

Yes. The form can be used for anything from a single room refresh to a larger decorating project.

Do you cover areas outside Wolverhampton?

Nearby surrounding areas can also be considered. Include your location or postcode so the job can be checked.

Can a quote be started from photos?

Photos can be helpful for understanding condition, preparation needs and access, especially for landlord and exterior work.

Do you handle preparation and making good?

Yes. Preparation work such as filling, sanding, priming and surface repairs can be factored into the quote where required.

Is this suitable for landlord or between-tenant refresh work?

Yes. The enquiry form is suitable for rental properties, practical refreshes and jobs where a clean presentation is important.
